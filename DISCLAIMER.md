### 1. 專案性質與用途

**Book Compiler** 是一個**知識整理工具**，目的是幫助使用者將研究結果組織成類似電子書的 Markdown 集合，方便閱讀、筆記與再利用。

產出的內容是 AI 根據公開 metadata（如 Open Library / Google Books 提供之書目資訊）、網路資料與模型知識整理而成的**摘要、評論或主題綜述**，屬於衍生性質的二次創作，**不能取代原書**。

若您想閱讀某本書的完整內容與原作者的論述，請購買該書或使用合法取得的版本。

### 2. 著作權與合理使用

使用本 skill 產出的內容可能涉及他人著作權。本專案提供以下建議（非法律建議，若有實際法律問題請諮詢律師）：

- **引用控制**：單次 blockquote 建議不超過 150 字，全書引用佔比不超過 10%，以符合多數司法管轄區的合理使用標準。
- **來源標示**：引用時附上書名、作者、出版年、章節或頁碼。
- **避免仿作**：預設採用旁白式敘事（「書中主張」「作者指出」），避免模擬原書作者口吻直接陳述觀點。
- **避免盜版來源**：不要從已知盜版網站抓取內容。
- **避免誤導**：不要宣稱產出是原書的授權版本、續作或官方改編。
- **強制重新命名**：本 skill 預設會重新命名書籍，不沿用使用者輸入的原書名，以降低商標與誤認風險。

### 3. 公版書模式

若想以最安全的方式使用本 skill，建議啟用 `--public-domain-only` 模式，僅處理可確認為公版的作品（通常是作者過世 ≥ 70 年、或美國 1928 年以前出版的作品）。

## English

### 1. Purpose

**Book Compiler** is a **knowledge-compilation tool** that helps users organize research into Markdown collections that resemble an e-book format, for reading, note-taking, and reuse.

This project is **not designed to replace purchasing books or to infringe any author's copyright**. Outputs are AI-generated **summaries, commentary, or thematic syntheses**, derived from publicly available metadata (Open Library / Google Books), web sources, and model knowledge. They are derivative works and **cannot substitute for the original books**.

### 2. Copyright & Fair Use

The following are **suggestions, not legal advice**. Consult a lawyer for actual legal questions.

- **Quote limits**: blockquotes ≤ 150 chars each, ≤ 10% of total book content.
- **Citations**: always cite title, author, year, chapter/page.
- **Avoid imitation**: default narrative is third-person commentary; do not impersonate the original author's voice.
- **Avoid piracy sources**: do not scrape from known pirate sites.
- **No misrepresentation**: do not claim outputs are authorized editions, sequels, or official adaptations.
- **Forced re-titling**: the skill always generates a new title; user input is not used as the final book title, reducing trademark/confusion risk.

### 3. Public-Domain Mode

For the safest use, enable `--public-domain-only` to restrict inputs to works in the public domain (generally: author deceased ≥ 70 years, or US works published before 1928).

### 4. User Responsibility

- Users are responsible for ensuring outputs comply with their local copyright law.
- Users must assess legal risk for commercial use. This project does **not** encourage commercial reproduction, imitation, or attributional fraud.
- When publishing outputs, retain the disclaimer and honestly label content sources (📖 book summary / 🌐 web / 🤖 AI).

### 5. Maintainer Disclaimer

- Maintainers accept **no legal liability** for user-generated content.
- The skill is provided **AS IS** with no warranties.
- DMCA notices will be handled per GitHub or platform procedure.

### 6. Inherent Limitations of AI Content

- AI models may produce errors, hallucinations, or outdated information.
- `🤖 [AI Generated]` sections have no independent source verification; treat with appropriate skepticism.
- Readers should verify important facts independently.

### 7. Reporting

- Copyright concerns: file a GitHub Issue or DMCA notice.
- Legal-risk concerns in the flow: PRs and Issues welcome.
